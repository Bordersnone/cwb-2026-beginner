## Handy Extras

- **[W3Schools Tryit Editor](https://www.w3schools.com/tryit/)** — blank sandbox, no setup needed
- **[W3C HTML Validator](https://validator.w3.org/#validate_by_input)** — paste your HTML, it finds your mistakes (missing closing tags!)

> 💡 When you can't remember how a tag works, look it up here *first*. Reading documentation is a developer skill — practice it today.
